=== Slide 6 ===
61508 Part 2, Table A.15 – Techniques & measures to control Systematic failures caused by HW design
6
Design diversity: Two or more items carrying out the same function but differing in design
Functional diversity: Two or more items carrying out different functions for same safety goal
Watchdog: A HW monitor confirms liveness of the HW that should be acting
Redundant compute: Distinct channels perform redundant computation, e.g. 2oo3 voting
